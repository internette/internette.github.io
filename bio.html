About Antoinette

Hey, I'm a front-end developer and designer based in Washington, D.C. I currently work as a developer at PBS Kids. I love experimenting with code, making the web do things it shouldn't do. When I'm not working, I'm attending local design and development events, exploring D.C. on foot, playing video games, or working on custom projects. In the past, I've built a Youtube Playlist extension and Khaledfy. I've also built out some smaller pieces, including Audio Visualizer, CSS Blend Mode Tester, and this Galaxy Randomizer. You can find me on Codepen, Dribbble, and Twitter. WordPress plus a custom theme made by me powers this site. Click here to check out my resume. It's printable!
